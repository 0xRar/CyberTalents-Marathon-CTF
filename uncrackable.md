## First Look
They gave us the file as a gzip lets unzip it first,

- **`gzip -d uncrackable.tar.gz`**

after unziping it we still get a tar,

- **`tar -xf uncrackable.tar`**

after extracting the tar we get a pdf file called: `sec2.pdf`
